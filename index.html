About Jacob

I fell in love with programming through text-based adventure games in the mid-90's. Those games were written in LPC. Antiquated as it is, I still enjoy visiting the old codebase from time to time.

After working and studying in Taiwan, I returned to Nashville to earn my Master of Education from Vanderbilt.

At Vanderbilt, I rediscovered my love of programming. When working on game-based computer-assisted educational research, I asked our lead developer how he created the games we used. He suggested that I enroll at Nashville Software School.

At Nashville Software School, I began learning to develop on the MEAN stack, which prepared me to do short-term freelance work with Secret Signal, a local boutique agency. There I expanded into Ruby on Rails and improved my front end Javascript skills.


At the moment...

Current Project: I am sharpening my skills in PHP and WordPress with some volunteer work with TechFed.

I am looking for new opportunities to develop. I am especially interested in research. If there's something I can help with, send me an email.
